About Me

Designing with
Purpose.

Nica Joy is a licensed pharmacist turned creative designer with a unique eye for detail shaped by both science and art. Her background in the health sciences gave her precision, discipline, and an analytical mindset ‚Äî qualities she now brings to every design project she takes on. Whether it's crafting a brand identity from scratch or designing publication materials that tell a compelling story, Nica approaches each project with the same care and intentionality that defined her work in the pharmaceutical field.

With a passion for visual storytelling and a growing portfolio spanning brand identity, multimedia design, and publication layouts, Nica creates work that is both beautiful and purposeful. She believes great design isn't just about aesthetics ‚Äî it's about communicating a message that moves people to action.
